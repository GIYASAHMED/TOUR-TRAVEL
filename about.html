About Us

Making Every Journey Memorable and Hassle-Free

Baishnavie Travels we believe that travel is the ultimate escape from the ordinary, an opportunity to create unforgettable experiences with your loved ones. Founded in 2009 by Arup Kumar Mali, our company specializes in providing exceptional travel services across Northeast India, covering destinations like Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Nagaland, Tripura, Manipur, and Mizoram. We also offer all-India tourism services to renowned locations such as Kashmir, Kolkata, Sikkim, Darjeeling, Mumbai, Uttarakhand, Shimla, Manali, Spiti, Himachal Pradesh, and even Nepal. With a fleet of over 30 vehicles, ranging from sedans to 26-seater Tempo Travellers, we ensure that your journey is comfortable and stress-free. Whether you’re traveling solo, with family, or as a group, our team is dedicated to offering a personalized and hassle-free travel experience, making every trip special.

Vision of the founder

The founder of Baishnavie Travlels, I, Arup Kumar mali, believe that travel is a way to explore, rejuvenate, and build cherished memories. Since 2009, my vision has been to provide our clients with exceptional travel experiences—whether they are exploring the natural beauty of Northeast India or the rich culture of other destinations across India. I am committed to ensuring that every journey is smooth, comfortable, and filled with joy, creating lasting experiences for all our travelers.

Since 2009, Baishnavie Travels has provided seamless travel experiences, especially across Northeast India and the rest of India, along with Nepal, offering comfortable vehicles and personalized services for a hassle-free journey.
