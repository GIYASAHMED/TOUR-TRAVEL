About Us

Making Every Journey Memorable and Hassle-Free

Ruchika Tour and Travels, we believe that travel is the ultimate escape from the ordinary, an opportunity to create unforgettable experiences with your loved ones. Founded in 2009 by Ranju Das, our company specializes in providing exceptional travel services across Northeast India, covering destinations like Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Nagaland, Tripura, Manipur, and Mizoram. We also offer all-India tourism services to renowned locations such as Kashmir, Kolkata, Sikkim, Darjeeling, Mumbai, Uttarakhand, Shimla, Manali, Spiti, Himachal Pradesh, and even Nepal. With a fleet of over 30 vehicles, ranging from sedans to 26-seater Tempo Travellers, we ensure that your journey is comfortable and stress-free. Whether you’re traveling solo, with family, or as a group, our team is dedicated to offering a personalized and hassle-free travel experience, making every trip special.
