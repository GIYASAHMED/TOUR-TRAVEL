About Us

Making Every Journey Memorable and Hassle-Free

Baishnavie Travels we believe that travel is the ultimate escape from the ordinary, an opportunity to create unforgettable experiences with your loved ones. Founded in 2009 by Arup Kumar Mali, our company specializes in providing exceptional travel services across Northeast India, covering destinations like Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Nagaland, Tripura, Manipur, and Mizoram. We also offer all-India tourism services to renowned locations such as Kashmir, Kolkata, Sikkim, Darjeeling, Mumbai, Uttarakhand, Shimla, Manali, Spiti, Himachal Pradesh, and even Nepal. With a fleet of over 30 vehicles, ranging from sedans to 26-seater Tempo Travellers, we ensure that your journey is comfortable and stress-free. Whether you’re traveling solo, with family, or as a group, our team is dedicated to offering a personalized and hassle-free travel experience, making every trip special.

Testimonial

Memorable Journeys, Happy Customers!

Pallabi Tiwari

"Cherrapunji blew me away with its natural wonders. Famous for being one of the wettest places on Earth, it offers dramatic landscapes with waterfalls, caves, and lush green valleys. The highlight of my trip was trekking to the Double Decker Living Root Bridge in Nongriat—an unforgettable experience. I also visited Nohkalikai Falls, which is India’s tallest plunge waterfall.

Krishna Kalita

"Tawang is a hidden gem nestled in the eastern Himalayas of Arunachal Pradesh. The journey to Tawang itself is breathtaking, with winding roads and snow-capped peaks. I visited the stunning Tawang Monastery—India’s largest—and felt deeply spiritual in its peaceful ambiance.

Rahul bezbarua

"Kaziranga National Park was a dream come true for me as a wildlife lover. The park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to the endangered one-horned rhinoceros. I took both jeep and elephant safaris, and spotted rhinos, elephants, and a variety of exotic birds. The guides were knowledgeable and ensured we followed all safety protocols. The open landscapes, dense grasslands, and wet marshes made the experience thrilling yet peaceful. The nearby lodges are eco-friendly and offer local Assamese cuisine. Kaziranga is a must-visit for anyone wanting an authentic, raw nature experience in Northeast India."

Muskan Barma

"My trip to Shillong was nothing short of magical. Known as the 'Scotland of the East', this charming hill station is full of natural beauty, friendly locals, and amazing weather. I visited Umiam Lake, Elephant Falls, and the vibrant Police Bazaar. The cool climate made walking through pine-lined streets a delight. What stood out to me was the cleanliness and the warm hospitality everywhere I went. Don Bosco Museum gave a deep insight into the culture of Northeast India. Shillong is a peaceful, scenic escape from city life and is perfect for solo travelers, couples, and families. Highly recommended!"

Since 2009, Baishnavie Travels has provided seamless travel experiences, especially across Northeast India and the rest of India, along with Nepal, offering comfortable vehicles and personalized services for a hassle-free journey.
