Assam

A Paradise of Nature, Wildlife, and Rich Heritage

Assam, a breathtaking eco-friendly destination, is known for its lush greenery, diverse wildlife, and vibrant culture. Home to the majestic one-horned rhino, five national parks, and the mighty Brahmaputra River, it offers adventure, spirituality, and world-famous tea. The Land of the Rising Sun and Timeless Beauty

Arunachal Pradesh, the Land of the Rising Sun, is a paradise of misty hills, waterfalls, and rich heritage. Sharing borders with China, Myanmar, and Bhutan, it boasts ancient monuments and vibrant tribal cultures. Mentioned in the Mahabharata and Kalika Purana, this land holds deep historical, spiritual, and cultural significance.

Arunachal Pradesh
Meghalaya

The Land of Clouds and Endless Wonders

Meghalaya, a paradise of misty hills and lush greenery, is among the wettest places on earth. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers, it offers trekking, caving, and wildlife spotting. Home to Khasi, Jaintia, and Garo tribes, its rich culture, breathtaking landscapes, and warm hospitality make it truly unforgettable.

The Land of Jewels and Rich Traditions

Manipur, meaning "the land of jewels," is a hidden gem in Northeast India, where vibrant traditions and unique lifestyles thrive. Known for its exquisite handloom textiles and renowned dance forms, this enchanting state prioritizes culture and nature, offering visitors a serene, beautiful escape into its rich heritage.

Manipur
Mizoram

The Land of Blue Mountains and Natural Beauty

Mizoram, the youngest state in Northeast India, is a paradise for travelers, featuring lush green hills, dense forests, crystal-clear waterfalls, and peaceful lakes. Known as the "Land of Blue Mountains," it celebrates a rich cultural heritage, marked by vibrant festivals, tribal origins, and a harmonious community spirit.

India's Rainbow State of Culture and Adventure

Nagaland, known as the rainbow state of India, features stunning landscapes filled with diverse flora and fauna. Located in Northeast India, it offers activities like trekking, rock climbing, and river rafting. With a rich tribal culture, Nagaland is predominantly Christian, showcasing vibrant traditions and famous festivals throughout the year.
